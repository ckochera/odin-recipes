Crusty Bread

Description

This is a classic no-knead bread recipe that is sure to impress your taste buds. Even more impressive is the small amount of work that results in huge taste.

The receipt below is sourced from Ken Forkish's book Flour Water Salt Yeast.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams white bread flour
  • 380 grams water, approximately 90-97 degrees Farenheit
  • 11 grams salt (I like to use Kosher salt)
  • 0.4 grams active dry yeast
